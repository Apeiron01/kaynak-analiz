Etsy Türkiye Commission Rates 2026 and Store Opening Guide

We clarify two critical questions in the same place for sellers who are considering opening Etsy from Türkiye: "How much is the commission?" and “Is it still possible to open a store?”. In this guide, we separate the fee items one by one and show you how to read the total cost realistically.

March 11, 2026 Cost and opening guide Etsy Türkiye

Quick Reply

Opening a store on Etsy from Türkiye seems possible by 2026; because Türkiye is on the Etsy Payments supported countries list. But the total deduction is not just "6.5% commission".

  • Listing fee: $0.20
  • Transaction fee: 6.5%
  • Türkiye Etsy Payments fee: 6.5% + 14 TRY
  • Türkiye Regulatory Operating Fee: 2.27%
  • Additional conditional items: deposit fee, currency conversion fee, Offsite Ads fee
Therefore, the question to ask is "how much is the commission?" but "Are the right product and the right price established at the total cost?" should be.

Can I Open a Store on Etsy from Türkiye?

Yes. In Etsy's official country list, Türkiye is among the Etsy Payments supported countries: Countries Eligible for Etsy Payments.

Etsy also states in the new shop onboarding flow that Etsy Payments is at the center and opening may be limited in some combinations of countries. Since Türkiye is on the list, opening is supported in the basic framework.

Two critical details:

  • Shop country and bank account country must be compatible.
  • Türkiye Etsy Payments sellers cannot accept PayPal.

Source: Seller verification and bank setup, How to get paid on Etsy.

Etsy Store Opening Flow (Short Queue for Türkiye)

On the Etsy side, opening is a technically simple process but closely tied to payment and verification. Starting point: etsy.com/sell.

  1. Create an Etsy account or log in.
  2. Select shop language, country and currency.
  3. Specify the store name.
  4. Create initial listings.
  5. Enter bank and payment information.
  6. Complete the verification steps.
  7. Launch the store.

It is better to start the boot from the desktop: How to Open an Etsy Shop.

Etsy Türkiye Commission Rates 2026: Basic Fees

1) Listing Fee

Etsy charges $0.20 for each new listing. A fee occurs even if there is no listing sale; In cases of renewal or multiple sales, the listing fee may come into play again.

Source: Listing fees and multiple quantities.

2) Transaction Fee

Etsy applies a 6.5% transaction fee on the order total. It's not just the product price; It may also include shipping and gift wrap items.

Source: Transaction fee details.

3) Türkiye Etsy Payments Processing Fee

Etsy's country-by-country tables list the payment processing fee for Türkiye as 6.5% + 14 TRY. This item works in addition to the 6.5% transaction fee.

Source: Etsy Payments fee table.

4) Türkiye Regulatory Operating Fee

There is also a 2.27% Regulatory Operating Fee for Türkiye. This item is a critical fee that many sellers overlook but affects margin.

Source: Regulatory Operating Fee.

Conditional Additional Fees in Türkiye

1) Shop Set-up Fee

Etsy may apply a one-time non-refundable set-up fee at startup. This amount may vary by country and is shown in the setup flow.

Source: Fees overview.

2) Deposit Fee

For Türkiye, a conditional deposit fee can be seen in the payout mechanism on Etsy Payments. Etsy tables list minimum, threshold and fixed fee items for Türkiye.

Source: Deposit fee details by country.

3) Currency Conversion Fee

If the listing currency and payment account currency are different, Etsy may apply an additional 2.5% conversion fee during conversion.

Source: Currency conversion on Etsy.

4) Offsite Ads Fee

Additional advertising interruption may occur for orders attributed to Offsite Ads. This does not apply to every order, but to orders originating from Offsite Ads.

Source: How Etsy's Offsite Ads Work.

How to Consider Total Outage on Etsy Türkiye?

Setting prices without stacking all items on an order basis will result in margin errors. The potential compensation basket generally works like this:

  • Listing fee
  • transaction fee
  • Payment processing fee
  • Regulatory operating fee
  • Currency conversion fee, if any
  • Offsite Ads fee, if any
  • Deposit fee if any

So the sentence "Etsy charges 6.5%" is technically correct, but incomplete by itself. In Turkey, fixed TRY items can quickly reduce the margin on low-priced products.

Critical Points of Attention When Opening from Türkiye

1) Set up the Country and Bank Information Correctly at First

The harmony of shop country and bank country is the basis of the opening; The assumption of post-correction is risky.

2) Make Currency Selection Operational

Incorrect currency setup can result in regular margin loss due to conversion fees.

3) Do not open the store with a single listing

It is not an official obligation; But a small but consistent initial set is better for user trust and conversion.

4) Don't Plan with the Expectation of PayPal

PayPal is not supported for Türkiye Etsy Payments sellers; Plan your payment setup accordingly.

5) Include All Deductibles in the Pricing Model

Calculate net profit based on platform deduction total, not just product cost.

Clear Result for Etsy Türkiye

It seems possible to open a store on Etsy from Türkiye in 2026. However, for sustainable results, the cost model must be established correctly as well as the opening.

Practical summary:

  • Listing fee: $0.20
  • Transaction fee: 6.5%
  • Türkiye processing fee: 6.5% + 14 TRY
  • Türkiye regulatory fee: 2.27%
  • Conditional items: setup, deposit, conversion, Offsite Ads

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I open a store on Etsy Türkiye?

Yes. Türkiye is among the supported countries in the Etsy Payments country list.

How much is the Etsy commission in Türkiye?

It is not a single item: listing, transaction, payment processing, regulatory fee and other contingent fees should be considered together.

Can PayPal be used on Etsy in Türkiye?

No. PayPal is not supported for Türkiye sellers on Etsy's payment infrastructure.

Is there a fixed set-up fee for store opening?

It could be. Etsy states that this item may vary depending on location and is shown in the setup flow.
