Quick Reply

What kind of education model does Lumina Academy offer?

Lumina Academy presents Etsy, Shopify, Meta Ads and Google Ads topics not as separate courses, but as a system connected in the order of application. The model progresses in three layers: first the basic concepts are clarified, then the application steps are opened on the screen, and in the last step the process is fixed with checklists. Therefore, the goal is not only to gain monitoring skills but also to gain direct application skills. The user first selects the training title, then determines the level, and then starts working in the appropriate module order. This flow reduces the information load and gives a clear path to the natural traffic visitor's question "where should I start?"

  • For a beginner's decision: Digital Marketing Level Test.
  • To switch to the training list: Academy Trainings.
  • For app-supported learning: Advertising Training Guide.

Frequently Asked Questions

These Are The Most Asked Questions By The Academy.

This section has been prepared to make decision making a little easier before opening the training pages.

Will the trainings be live?

Training will progress with the support of both live lessons and video content that can be watched later. Even if the user misses the live lesson, he will be able to watch it again.

Will it be entry level only?

No. Startup, middle and application layers are considered separately. But the first contents that open will focus on establishing the basic order.

How to link to the blog?

Relevant blog content will be linked to each training title. Thus, the user will be able to proceed between the course and the content in a single flow.
